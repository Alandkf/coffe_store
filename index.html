About Us

At Suli coffee shop, we believe coffee is more than just a drink—it’s an experience. Located in the heart of Sulaimaniyah, we serve ethically sourced, expertly roasted coffee alongside fresh pastries and delicious bites. Whether you're here for your morning boost or a cozy catch-up with friends, our warm and welcoming space is the perfect place to unwind. Come in, take a sip, and make yourself at home!
